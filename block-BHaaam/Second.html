Middle Class Family

Majority of the middle-class family suffered a lot in India, due to unemployment, illiteracy. People have to face an abundant of tearful, agony, miserable tough life, tension, and fear because of poverty. This brings frustration and depression in life. Their parents do not know that there are so many careers options other than specific field. While facing mental pressures from the beginning, they are pressurize to become engineer, doctor or have to clear civil services examination.

What they really do to overcome failure & problem?

  • To overcome this failure & problem, they approach a priest for knowing their future.
  • They follow directions blindly given by the priest. It is just like a daily routine for them to visit astrologer.
  • According to them, they will help with an appropriate prediction that can show the right directions of life.

Beliefs Followed by Middle-Class Families.

  • Wearing useless rings in fingers at specific days
  • Cutting nails and hairs are not allowed on specific days that bring failure in life.
  • Don’t shake legs or arms abnormally; he or she will definitely lose wealth
  • Eyes twitching bring success.
  • They force their children to go for engineering and Government jobs
  • Regularly visit to temple for the solution of problem

Main Goal Of Human Life

Faith in God and prescribed duty (karma) with an unselfish mind is considered as the main goal of human life. Material desires and attainment of joyful life shouldn’t be the highest goal of human life. All beings are enforced to performed action in life. We should always focus on our duties rather than fruitful results. One should never hesitate while doing their duties because there’s nothing important than one’s personal duty in life. So get up ready to perform because nothing is easy in life. It is better to die for responsibilities and duties than to live an ordinary life without doing anything and ultimately die

Always treats your pleasure and pain, gain and loss, victory and defeat alike in life. By doing your duty this way, you will never feel regret in life. The fruits of work should not be the motive. Perform your duty (karma) in life to the best of your strength as the order given by God without consideration for the personal pleasure or the fruits of your work.
